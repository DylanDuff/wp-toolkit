# Managing Content

Some content on this site — like Services, Team Members, or similar sections — isn't edited visually. Instead, it's managed through structured forms in the WordPress admin. This keeps things consistent and makes updates straightforward.

---

## Finding Your Content

Each content type has its own item in the left-hand admin menu. Look for entries like **Services**, **Projects**, or **Team Members** depending on what's been set up for your site.

Click on one to see a list of all existing entries.

---

## Adding a New Entry

1. Click **Add New** at the top of the list.
2. Enter a **Title** at the top — this is usually the name of the item (e.g. "Landscape Design", "Jane Smith").
3. Fill in the fields below the title. These will vary depending on the content type.
4. Click **Publish** when you're ready for it to go live, or **Save Draft** to come back to it later.

---

## Editing an Existing Entry

1. Click the entry title from the list.
2. Update the relevant fields.
3. Click **Update** to save — changes go live immediately.

---

## Types of Fields You'll See

| Field | How to use it |
|---|---|
| **Text** | Just type in the box |
| **Text area** | Multi-line text, no formatting |
| **Rich text** | Formatted text — bold, lists, links, etc. |
| **Image** | Click to open the media library and choose or upload an image |
| **File** | Same as image, but for documents like PDFs |
| **Toggle** | A switch to turn something on or off |
| **Dropdown / Radio** | Pick from a list of preset options |
| **Repeater** | A set of rows you can add, reorder, or remove — see below |

### Working with Repeaters

Some fields let you add multiple rows — like a list of team members or a set of pricing tiers.

- Click **Add Row** to add a new one
- Drag the handle on the left to reorder rows
- Click **Remove** to delete a row

> Deleting a row is permanent once you save. Double-check before removing anything.

---

## Drafts, Publishing & Hiding Entries

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Publish** | Makes the entry visible on the site |
| **Save Draft** | Saves your work without making it live |
| **Update** | Saves changes to something already published |

To temporarily hide a published entry, open it, change the **Status** to **Draft** in the top-right panel, and click **Update**.

---

## Deleting an Entry

From the list view, hover over the entry and click **Trash**. The entry is removed from the site immediately.

To permanently delete it, click the **Trash** link at the top of the list and choose **Delete Permanently**.

> If an entry is referenced elsewhere on the site, removing it may cause missing content in that area. Check with your developer if you're unsure.
